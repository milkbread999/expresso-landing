Our Vision

At Expresso, we believe that confidence in public speaking is invaluable, both professionally and personally. We're a high school student-led startup with the aim of helping people along their communication journeys.

Currently, we seek to provide customized, free public speaking training to as many people as possible through asynchronous feedback sessions, speaking exercises, consultations, speech writing support, and more.

In the future, we're looking towards developing an AI-based speech analysis platform that eases people into public speaking training through bite-sized drills.
